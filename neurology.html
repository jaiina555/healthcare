Neurology Department

The Neurology Department focuses on diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. From headaches and migraines to complex conditions like epilepsy, stroke, and Parkinson's disease, our team offers expert care. Advanced diagnostic tools, including MRIs and CT scans, help identify issues accurately, ensuring effective treatments. With a patient-centered approach, neurologists collaborate with other specialists to provide holistic care. Our commitment is to improve patients' quality of life, addressing neurological concerns with compassion and expertise.
