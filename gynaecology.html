Gynaecology Department

The Gynaecology Department specializes in women's health, addressing concerns from adolescence to menopause and beyond. With a focus on reproductive health, the department provides services such as routine check-ups, fertility counseling, pregnancy care, and menopause management. Advanced diagnostic tools and treatments ensure accurate care for conditions like PCOS, fibroids, and hormonal imbalances. Our compassionate team of gynecologists and nurses offers personalized care, prioritizing comfort and well-being. Whether it's preventive care or complex surgeries, we are committed to empowering women with the best healthcare solutions.
